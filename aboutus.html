About us


Our Mission Statement

Our mission statement is as follows;

"Our mission is to value every culture we have on our team and to represent that no matter the differences between us, we are the same hearted people.""





Our Story

Started in 2023, Banshee Bites is small enterprise, comprised of seven members, ranging from countries from Ireland to India. Our goal is to provide yummy food along with celebrating each others wonderful cuisines.


We cook all our food to order so you can enjoy our goods straight away.




We make all of our own cakes, breads, scones, and other baked goods. We want to provide fresh, high-quality, and healthy food at an affordable price. Check out our allergen-info page and desserts page for a complete list of our products and ingredients.
